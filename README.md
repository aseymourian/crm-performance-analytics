# CRM & Marketing Performance Analytics Model
### Leasing & Revenue Optimization | Boston Residential Portfolio

## Overview
A fully designed CRM data architecture and analytics model built to evaluate 
lead conversion, campaign performance, and customer segmentation across a 
Boston-area residential leasing portfolio. The model is built on real-world 
leasing operations experience and demonstrates how integrated CRM data can 
drive smarter pricing, targeting, and marketing decisions.

> This dataset is simulated and does not contain real customer or company data.
> The CRM structure, workflows, KPIs, and analysis are based on real-world 
> experience managing leasing operations, lead conversion, and marketing 
> performance.

---

## Data Architecture

The model is structured across 9 relational sheets, each representing a core 
business object:

| Sheet | Description |
|---|---|
| **Contacts** | One row per person — segment, role, and contact preferences |
| **Accounts** | Schools, hospitals, agencies, and partner organizations |
| **Properties** | One row per building — submarket, type, and unit mix |
| **Units** | One row per rentable unit — occupancy, pricing, and renovation level |
| **Listings** | One row per listing period by platform (Zillow, Apartments.com, MLS, etc.) |
| **Campaigns** | Email campaigns, paid listings, partner outreach, and A/B tests |
| **Leads** | One row per inquiry — source, stage, objections, and outcome |
| **Activities** | Every touchpoint — calls, tours, follow-ups, applications, and leases |
| **Leases** | Closed deals tied to leads, units, and revenue |

## Key Analysis Areas

**Lead Conversion & Funnel Analysis**
Full leasing pipeline tracked from first inquiry through signed lease across 
650+ leads. Conversion rates analyzed by lead source group (email campaign, 
third-party listing, partner referral, direct/organic) and qualify status.

**Campaign Performance**
18 campaigns tracked across email nurture, paid listings, partner outreach, 
A/B tests, and event promotion. Performance measured by channel, audience 
segment, budget, and target KPI (open rate, CTR, inquiry rate, tour bookings).

**Customer Segmentation**
460+ contacts segmented by institution (Tufts Medical, Tufts Dental, New 
England School of Law, Graduate Students, Working Professionals), contact role, 
and preferred contact method. Primary vs. non-target segments flagged for 
prioritization.

**Pricing & Occupancy Analysis**
64 units across 12 properties tracked by submarket (Boston Core, Dorchester, 
West Roxbury), unit type, renovation level, 2024 vs. 2025 asking rent, and 
occupancy status. Supports pricing precision and vacancy reduction strategy.

**Platform & Listing Performance**
156 listing periods across Zillow, Apartments.com, MLS, Realtor.com, and YGL 
tracked by days on market, asking rent, featured status, and listing outcome.

**Closed Loss Analysis**
Lost leads tagged by reason (price too high, location mismatch, timing, chose 
competitor, building condition, no response) to identify conversion barriers 
and inform operational and marketing improvements.
